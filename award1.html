Cum Laude Trophy

The Women of the Cum Laude Awards are open to all women aged 35 or under (candidates must be aged 35 or under on December 31st 2020). There are three Special Awards that operate outside of this criteria:

  • Women Vastly involved in volunteer work

  • Women actively involved in improving education

  • Women Vastly involved in societal interventions
