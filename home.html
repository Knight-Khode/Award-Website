Brief Overview Of Our Awards

What We Look For

We look for once in a generation women who play significant strides in the world of politics, empowering younger generations, providing soical interventions and wielding the power of contributing to the world of technology. Our awards are merit based and competitors are thoroughly measured according to our award scale. On an overall scale we are focused on providing gratification to women that are uniquely impacting the society.
