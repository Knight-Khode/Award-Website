Sports Trophy

Women make up 40% of sportspeople, however as of 2020 they continue to only receive 4% of the total sports media coverage in print and broadcast devoted to them. This is even despite fluctuations during key events like the Olympics or World Cup. The sports industry continues to dim the spotlight on females however this has never undermined the work rate of certain industrous women. For this matter, the Sports Award was formulated to keenly identify relentless sports women and place them underneath the spotlight they deserve.
